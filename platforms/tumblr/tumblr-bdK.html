tibit bookmarklet

How to use this bookmarklet generator:

  1. Enter your Bitcoin pay address in the field above
  2. Hit submit
  3. Click and drag the "tibit bookmarklet" link to your bookmarks
  4. Browse to tumblr, and bring up the post editor (either by creating a new post or editing an existing one)
  5. With the edit frame open, click your tibit bookmarklet, and a text link should automatically be appended to the end of your post.
