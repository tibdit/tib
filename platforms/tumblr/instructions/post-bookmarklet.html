This will automatically place a text-based button into the end of your post for you - this text button can be seen from the tumblr dashboard, not just within your individual tumblr page/theme. The display of this text button within your editor will depend on what we will refer to as your "Editor Mode." There are 3 available modes; Rich Text (usually the Default,) HTML, and Markdown. You may check the mode your editor is currently in by clicking the cog in the upper-right of your editor pane, and seeing the "Text editor" property (as in 1 and 2 below:)

In the case of Markdown and HTML editor modes, a HTML code will be inserted. If you browse to "Preview" within your edit window, you will see the button as it will appear to your readers. In the case of Rich Text mode, the button will be displayed immediately as it will appear to your readers. It should look like this:
