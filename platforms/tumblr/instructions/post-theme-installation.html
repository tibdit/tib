With your bookmarklet created and set-up, perhaps with a couple of text buttons included in your tumblr posts, you might now want to further integrate tibit into your tumblr theme. This will give you access to our dynamic SVG buttons, like this one:

Aside from being prettier to look at, these dynamic buttons will track your tibs and display a counter, taking into account any text buttons added to the same post, hiding them and including any tibs made to the counter. They'll also react to tibs, changing from "tib" to "tibbed".

1. Go to theme HTML

  • Go to your tumblr dashboard
  • Click the account icon (pictured below)
  • Click “Edit appearance” under the appropriate tumblr
  • Click “Edit theme” under “Website Theme”
  • Click “Edit HTML” in the top left

2. Add the Javascript initialiser script

  • Click inside the HTML frame to place your cursor (1)
  • Press Ctrl+F (Cmd+F on Mac) to bring up the search bar
  • Type: “/head” (2)
  • Add script snippet just above/before </head> tag (see (3) above - the bitcoin address highlighted is the public address of one of the team who uses it on their tumblr blog so don’t use this or they’ll get your tibs)
<script src='https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/script.js/2.5.8/script.min.js'></script>
<script>
    $script('http://widget.tibdit.com/assets/js/tib-2.4.js', function() {
        tibInit( {
            'PAD' : 'mytibs9YhLYtrVhQkmTdbDS51H54WyrxTx'
            'BTN' : 'bubble',
            'PLT' : 'tumblr'
        });
    });
</script>

3. Add the button

  • Click within the HTML pane, and hit Ctrl+F/Cmd+F again to bring up the search bar
  • This time, type “{Body}” (1)
  • This should bring up one or more results - these “{Body}” instances represent the content of your tumblr posts. The latter will likely be the instance you want (Non-reblogged, as opposed to reblogged, posts.) (2)
  • Paste the following button snippet below the {Body} block(s) (or anywhere else in your theme you might want to include a button)
    • {block:NotReblog}<button type='button' class='bd-tib-btn' data-bd-SUB='{PostID}' data-bd-TIB='{permalink}'></button>{/block:NotReblog}

4. Save

  • Open your tumblr in a new tab/window ( your-blog.tumblr.com )
  • Return to your original edit window/tab
  • Click Update Preview button at top of HTML edit panel
  • Click Save button at top of HTML edit panel
  • Return to your tumblr - you should now see a tib button on each individual non-reblog post

5. Done

  • Live tib buttons should be visible at the bottom of original posts.

If you select a size and then you don’t how it appears on your blog or a colour that doesn’t match, you can always redo these steps at any point.
